Q1. Which Google API makes your app work in 100+ languages?
✅ Google Translate auto-detects the user's language, you translate their question to English for Claude, get the answer, then translate back. One integration, the entire world.
Q2. What pattern allows you to answer questions from a student's own PDF textbook?
✅ RAG: split the document into ~500 word chunks, find the most relevant ones for each question (keyword or semantic search), inject them into Claude's prompt, ask Claude to answer from those chunks only. Scalable, accurate, and transparent.
